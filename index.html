Thank you for your interest in Track COVID at Home! This health study is being conducted so that researchers can learn about how COVID-19 is passed among people who live together. Westat, a research company based in Rockville, Maryland, is managing the study. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is sponsoring the study.

For this research, it is important to get information from the person that has a positive COVID-19 test and all of their household members, as soon as possible after being tested. To find out if you're eligible, click on "Get Started." If you'd like to learn more about this study, click on "About" or "FAQs."
